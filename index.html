Welcome to Northrop & Johnson Asia-Pacific

Northrop & Johnson Asia-Pacific at a glance

Since 1949, Northrop & Johnson has offered a new dimension of sophistication and service to the yachting lifestyle. Northrop & Johnson’s reputation of success is built on decades of commitment to clients and commerce within the industry. From yacht sales and purchase to charter to management and marketing of the world’s most discerning luxury vessels, Northrop & Johnson offers a total-service approach.

Northrop & Johnson is proud to have a strong presence in the Asia-Pacific region. Our flagship office is located in Phuket, Thailand, hub of the yachting industry in the region. Our dedicated brokers and staff are region experts, either born and raised in Southeast Asia or having lived in the region for decades. Besides an exceptional team, we offer unrivaled service, and unmatched resources.

Exclusive Yachts for Sale in Asia

Our selection of quality, pre-owned vessels

Northrop & Johnson APAC also provides full brokerage and new-build services.

Northrop & Johnson APAC also provides full brokerage and new-build services. We assist you in finding your ideal yacht for purchase or selling your current yacht. We even manage construction projects from start to finish, whether you’re looking to build the luxury yacht of your dreams, or refit an existing yacht to your specifications. Our dedicated yacht brokers are renowned for being experts in their field in Southeast Asia and beyond. We ensure your experience with Northrop & Johnson is nothing short of excellent.

Yachts For Charter

Private Yacht Charter: A Safe Holiday

During your private yacht charter, you can have as much or as little interaction with the ‘outside world’, exactly as you want it. Relax on board, discover secluded beaches, swim in clear turquoise waters, and enjoy the water toys your yacht has available. Eat ashore or delight in excellent food on board, freshly prepared by your yacht’s chef.

During your yachting holiday on a luxury Superyacht, surrounded by stunning views, you and your guests are most likely the only ones for miles around.

Charter Yachts: A Cruise Away From It All

Luxury charter yachts have high standards of safety and security, on par or higher than most luxury hotels and resorts. Accommodating 6 to 12 guests, your Captain and crew can dedicate themselves to delivering outstanding service and ensuring your safety and comfort. A luxury boating holiday offers privacy and safety, and an extraordinary adventure!

Destinations: Safe and Secluded

We can recommend beautiful cruising locations in Southeast Asia, including a wealth of unexplored islands. Perfect for a tropical holiday away from crowds, enjoy a luxury boating holiday and explore uninhabited islands at your leisure, go beachcombing, swimming, snorkeling, diving, or relax onboard.
